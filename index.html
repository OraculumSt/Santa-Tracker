Information about Santa's journey

Santa Claus on December 24 at 9:30 a.m. (UTC) comes out of his hut to deliver presents all over the world in just 24 hours!
So that good children will find their presents under their Christmas tree on the day of December 25.
With this map you and your family can follow Santa's journey live and who knows maybe he will even show up shining brightly in the sky!
